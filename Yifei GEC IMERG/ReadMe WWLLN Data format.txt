
WWLLN .loc files (UT time): 
year/month/day, hr:min:sec, latitude, longitude, error, number of stations
e.g., 2013/11/07,00:00:00.181689, 23.7676, -86.0661, 13.0, 14


WWLLN tropical storm location .txt files:
This is a .txt file of WWLLN locations within ± 10 degrees Lat/Lon of 
the storm center, formatted as follows:
UT-year, UT-month, UT-day, UT-hour, UT-min, UT-sec, latitude, longitude, 
East-West distance from storm center (km), North-South Distance from storm center (km)
